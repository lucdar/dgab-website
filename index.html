About Us

Dance Games at Berkeley exists to foster a community around and through arcade style dance games like Dance Dance Revolution, Pump It Up, and In the Groove. The club also manages and maintains the Dance Dance Revolution machine in MLK Student Union. The club and machine are open to all people of all skill levels and experience.

The Machine is open whenever the UC Berkeley MLK Jr. Student Union is open. Their hours are available here.

Rules

  1. Respect equipment and the environment
    • Be Gentle with the pads, they are modified to be extra-sensitive, so aggressive stomping isn't necessary.
    • Be careful with food and drink by the machine.
  2. Respect other players and other people in the Student Union
    • Our community has zero-tolerance for bigotry or hate of any kind. If you ever feel unwelcome, let any officer know and we'll address it accordingly.
    • Respect the queue and don't manipulate it unfairly.
    • Keep your noise levels to a reasonable level. It can be disruptive to players and other people who we share the space with.
  3. Have fun :)
